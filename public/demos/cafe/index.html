Our Story

Mila was born out of a simple obsession: coffee should never be ordinary. We source single-origin beans directly from farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, roasted weekly in small batches in our Dublin roastery.

Our pastries are baked fresh every morning. Our space was designed for the kind of slow mornings that make cities worth living in. Every detail — from the playlist to the pour — is intentional.
